About Me

Hello there! My name is Marvin and I am an ui/ux designer and developer from Germany. My journey as a designer and developer began in 2014, when I decided to start studying science in the field of computer visualization and design (CVD). Sounds fancy, right? Well, it is. CVD is a bachelor-degree course that was created to teach students all the parts of design and informatics that correlate so that they can comprehend how to design and develop a user interface that is visually appealing, easy to use and ready to be deployed as an app of any kind.

Throughout the degree we’ve been taught to design and develop user interfaces from front to back. Meaning that we can guide an app all the way from the first idea to the live-release. Therefore I know how to find and evaluate the idea for a new feature or a whole product through research, personas, moodboards and red routes. But that’s just the first step. The next is to create user journey maps, wireframes and prototypes which will be used to generate a pattern library and prepare for the user interface development with a feature matrix.

To develop an user interface I usually use Vue.js as a framework but am also experienced with Angular. The styles are written in sass or less with the methodology of BEM which I’ve successfully applied to some projects as of now. When it comes to coding logic I’m most familiar with JavaScript but I took my time to try TypeScript in a project and am eager to get better at it.

Yes, that’s me. Let me tell you a little bit about me and what I do after work. Besides designing, developing and drinking coffee I am also a social and sporty guy. I keep hustling on the weekend though, playing the bass for the melodic power metal band Reinforcer. If you ever see me on stage you'll notice that I look a lot meaner than I actually am. Well, that's just because it's metal.
